About me

I’m a science and environmental journalist and audio producer based in the Midwest. As a freelancer, I've written news and feature stories for National Geographic, Scientific American, Smithsonian, Science, Hakai, Undark and other outlets. I'm also an audio producer and and reporter for NASA’s flagship agency podcast, Curious Universe.

I write a semi-regular Substack newsletter, The Science Reporter's Cut, that pulls back the curtain on science journalism.

Get in touch

I'm always available for freelance assignments, especially covering Earth systems science, climate change, and the history and culture of science and its intersection with society.

If you're a scientist working on something cool, an editor looking for a writer or a journalist covering science or the environment, I'd love to connect! Reach out on social media or via my contact form. And if you're interested in following my work, you can subscribe on the contact page to receive new articles via email.
