Mello

Mello , a family owned small business, is committed to creating timeless clothing for children.

Our items are made with traditional methods and are shipped directly to your home. At Mello, we prioritize ethical and sustainable clothing production, making us a brand that parents can trust. Our goal is to help parents fill their child's wardrobe with clothes that are classic, but lively

P3 was created in March of 2024 with the goal of rescuing Pit Bulls and Pit Bull type dogs from overcrowded shelters where they are at risk of euthanasia and providing the necessary training for them to provide emotional support, nightmare alerts, and pressure therapy during episodes of anxiety and stress so they can be paired with a Veteran struggling with PTSD reducing the risk of Veteran suicide.

Our sizes run from infants all the way up through juniors, to serve families with children of all ages.
